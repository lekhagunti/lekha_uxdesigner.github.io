Quick cure: A platform that connects you with doctors and pharmacists

App description

Quick cure is a platform that brings Doctors, patients and pharmaceuticals together via an application. Sometimes we don’t find time to visit doctors for small issues in such a case we look for quick treatments. Quick cure resolves such issues by making it possible for the users to communicate with the doctors who can resolve their problem and share the prescription if given with the pharmaceuticals near their location to collect the medication, this makes the process of getting cured quicker.
Note: "The patients are advised not to depend completely on the application during any severe health issues and rather visit their doctors immediately."

Warmth: A platform to travel emotional dilemmas together

App description

Warmth is a mobile application where you can communicate with people to share your emotions, dilemmas you are undergoing, ask queries and take or give advices, also share your experience in similar situations.


User flow

The image shown below is the user flow for this app, it shows various tabs and options that user will follow in the process of using this application.


Onboarding screens

There are five onboarding screens as shown below the first two being the logo screen and welcome screen respectively. Next comes the sign up screen where you can sign up directly or via Google or Facebook accounts. The fourth is the optional screen where you are required to fill few basic personality details.


Home screen

There are 4 variations of home screen that is as you are on home screen you get to see your profile image for few seconds later the quotes toggle periodically. Home screen has 5 tabs Share emotion, My chats, Group posts, Join group and Inspire me tabs.


'Share emotion' tab's interface

Share emotion is the first tab in the home screen on clicking it leads to the page where different groups you can join in order to share your emotions that are available, you must choose the group that matches the kind of emotion you are willing to share. Further on joining the group a chat interface opens where one can only post their emotion or join one’s group to give advice or share their relevant experiences.


Home screen tab’s interfaces

These are the tabs on home screen when opened individually i.e My chats, Group posts, Join group and Inspire me. My chats has all the groups you created or you joined in order to communicate your emotions. Group posts has all the groups you joined and you get to see the posts made by others and join appropriate one. Join group allows you to join the group you would like to be part off and Inspire me is the tab that allows you to read various quotes belonging to different categories and get inspired.


'Inspire me' tab in detail

Inspire me tab on home screen opens into a list of categories of quotes which on clicking the user can see quotes relating to the respective category.

Pedopt: Web application that connects you to animal shelters and pet doners to adopt pet or donate pet

App description

Pedopt is a Web application which stages animal shelters and pet doners so that people willing to adopt these animals can easily find and contact them for adoption. It also helps people who want to donate their pets due to their personal reasons to find eligible owners for their pets.


User flow

The image shown below is the user flow for this app, it shows various tabs and options that user will follow in the process of using this application. Starting with the onboarding then home screen which has two tabs- Adopt and donate.
Adopt leads to an interface where you can search animal shelter and pet doners in your location and select the pets you would like to adopt accordingly.
Donate leads to a page with form you are required to fill in order to post donation of pet.


Onboarding screens

There are two main onboarding screens i.e the Welcome screen and registration page or is already have an account there is a direct login page then comes the home screen with two tabs- Adopt and Donate. The image shown below are designs for both web and mobile devices.


Animal shelter adoption process

The screen shown below are the designs for the animal shelters adoption process where there are categories of animals and further the images of the animals for adoption which on clicking opens to a page with various health and other details of the animal and at the end there is Adopt button which on clicking opens an overlay of details of the person you must contact in order to move forward with the adoption process.


Adoption from Pet doner

These are almost similar to that of animal shelter interfaces here the pet for adoption are available which on further selection opens into a page with details of the pet and also the contact of the pet owner is available


Pet donation process

When Donate tab is chosen in the home screen it leads to a page with optipn to choose whether the user is running an animal shelter or is pet doner further on selecting the type of user, users are lead to a page with registration form accordingly which has to be filled in order to create donation post, where there are various inputs like pet health status and also the reason why th they are donating their pets and even they are required ton upload at least three images of the animal for donation.
