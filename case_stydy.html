CASE STUDY- 1

Warmth: A platform to travel emotional dilemmas together

Problem statement

Every day we undergo through so many emotions, problems and dilemmatic situations where we take suggestions and advices from people we trust like family, friends and colleagues. But sometimes we want these suggestions or advices from people who have undergone through similar situations and maybe we want to share emotions with people whom we don’t know. In such conditions we definitely can use the internet as a perfect platform to find new people with whom we can ccommunicate in order to overcome through our problems, but this may results in security issues and fraudulent experiences.
To solve this issue to an extent I have come up with the application named warmth where we can communicate with strangers who have undergone similar situation and share our emotions or even give advices or suggestion to others without revealing your details apart from name.


User description

  • Age group:16 & above
  • Qualification:Qualified enough to understand the motive of the application.
  • Occupation:Doesn’t matter unless they have certain motive to use the application in a good way.
  • Geographic location:This app is made considering the users are Indians but can be used globally too.
  • Psychological state of the users:This App is majorly associated with the user's psychology because the users share emotions that they are not basically willing to share with their loved ones or acquaintances rather are willing to share with strangers who have undergone similar emotions. Users are in psychologically disturbed state who are looking out for help from people similar to themselves.
  • Motive of the user:Any user who feels to share his/ her emotions of all kinds or would like to get or give suggestions and advices in their area of interests are potential users of the Warmth Application.

Challenge faced

The main challenge faced while designing this application is how to make the communication between the users secured, where the user who wants help from others or want to share his/ her opinions and feelings, he/ she should be able to do the same safely without revealing personal details unwillingly. I was able to overcome this challenge by designing sharing emotion as a post rather than normal chat process so that only people who are interested in helping the person can join the group and continue their communication in a separate group which results in first level of security and further who joins the group and communicates cannot see the group head or any other participant's details apart from their names.



CASE STUDY- 2

Pedopt: Web application that connects ypu to animal shelters and pet doners to adopt pet or donate pet


Problem statement

Animals cannot speak like humans but they can express unconditional love better than humans, this is a fact. Also, according to studies, having a pet reduces stress in our lives to a great extent. There are so many dogs and cats on our streets which thrive for their lives, get injured, starve and eventually die. Some of them find their way to animal shelters and lead the rest of their lives without any purpose. on the otherhand every person who wants a pet looks for costly and prestigious breeds, pay huge prices and buy them ,no offence though. But animals those in shelters never get to experience true human love and care. Some people rather are ready to adopt such animals from shelters or from doners who give away their pets due to various reasons. For such people there must be a platform where they can find the animal centers and pet doners around them, verify and then adopt the pet they need.
Pedopt is a Web application which stages animal shelters and pet doners so that people willing to adopt these animals can easily find and contact them for adoption. It also helps people who want to donate their pets due to their personal reasons to find eligible owners for their pets.


User description

  • Age group:Any (who are willing to adopt pet)
  • Qualification:Qualified enough to understand the motive of the application.
  • Occupation:Doesn’t matter unless they have certain motive to use the application in a good way.
  • Geographic location:This app is made considering the users are Indians but can be used globally.
  • Psychological state of the users:This App is for people who want a pet and can get emotionally connected to them.
  • Motive of the user:All users who are willing to adopt pets from animal shelters or from pet doners and who want to donate their pet for various reasons.

    Challenge faced

    Pedopt is a simple web application, the only challenge i faced was to show the pet doners their donation post without creating private account for the users. This is because pedopt is an informational application that only provides to stage your adoption or donation and not a complete functional application. I was able to overcome this challenge by introducing My post tab in the settings by clicking it further opens a page that shows the donations the user has made and on the other hand if the user hasn't posted any donation of animals at all it will show No Donation posts yet. This made it possible to show the donations made by the user without creating a private account for it.
